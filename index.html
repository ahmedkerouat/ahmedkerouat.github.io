Why a Gas Mask ?

In my artwork and on the Internet I like to depict myself using a gas mask. Because it's not who I am that matters, it's the reflection of my actions and my philosophy that hold a significance.

Our world grapples with numerous issues today, I harness the power of art to criticize society and address these problems. In most of my work, I aim to evoke strong emotions, intending to shock and, in turn, inspire thoughts and actions. "Anyone can wear the mask" - Miles Morales

Somno Ignis (2022)

View on GitHub

Inspired by a mini-game in Minecraft called BedWars, where the goal is to defend your bed against an enemy team, in this video game I coded in Python using the Pygame library, you incarnate a fire spirit that defends its bed against a horde of monsters that won't let it rest and get sleep.

Atom Simulator (2022)

View on GitHub

This project utilizes a sophisticated sphere packing technique to simulate all 118 elements based on the Bohr Model. It provides detailed electron configuration information generated in the code for rendering and allows you to manipulate atom stability. Additionally, it features a camera for enhanced interaction.
