About Me

I'm a 17-year-old with dreams and a passion for various aspects of life. I find joy in art, coding, and leadership.

I like to represent myself using a gas mask. It's not just a symbol, but a metaphor. Society can sometimes be toxic, and the mask reminds us that anyone can identify with it. It's a symbol of unity, a reminder that everybody can wear the mask.

In life, everything is about what you decide to do. I strive to give my best in everything I pursue.
