Why a Gas Mask ?

In my artwork and on the Internet I like to depict myself using a gas mask. Because it's not who I am that matters, it's the reflection of my actions and my philosophy that hold a significance.

Our world grapples with numerous issues today, I harness the power of art to criticize society and address these problems. In most of my work, I aim to evoke strong emotions, intending to shock and, in turn, inspire thoughts and actions. "Anyone can wear the mask" - Miles Morales
